The 10-Second Jailbreak: The Helicopter Escape of Joel David Kaplan

by Paul Kirchner (Author)

A daring escape from the worst prison on earth

In this gripping true story, Paul Kirchner chronicles the audacious escape of Joel David Kaplan, a businessman wrongfully imprisoned in Mexico’s notorious Santa Martha Acatitla prison. With help from his resourceful sister and a team of daring accomplices, Kaplan executed one of the most dramatic prison breaks in history—escaping via helicopter in broad daylight.

Kirchner details the elaborate planning, close calls, and split-second decisions that made the "10-Second Jailbreak" a success. This book offers a thrilling account of ingenuity, resilience, and the lengths to which people will go to seek justice and freedom. Perfect for fans of true crime and high-stakes adventure.

Beyond the escape itself, Kirchner delves into the aftermath of Kaplan's daring flight, including the international manhunt that followed and the legal and political intrigue surrounding his case. The book sheds light on the broader implications of corruption and injustice within the Mexican prison system, making it a compelling read for those interested in the intersection of crime, politics, and human rights.
